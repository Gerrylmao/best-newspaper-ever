Grid assignment

As some of us may already know, some the current art classes are taking an assignment on "the grid method"
This method consists of replicating an image with squares as guides. This means that you will replicate square by square of an image to finally have it almost 100% exactly the same.

Teen Mental Health Awareness

May 2nd is the World's teen mental wellness day. To demonstrate this posters have been put up all over school. These posters bring awareness to this problem that many teenagers have to face.Teen Mental Health Awareness Month is all about showing teens that it's okay to not be okay. It's a chance to talk openly about mental health, learn how to handle tough emotions, and support friends who might be struggling.
